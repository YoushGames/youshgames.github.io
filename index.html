Privacy Policy

Paint Pool · by Yoush Games

Effective date: 27 June 2026 Publisher: Yoush Games

Paint Pool (“the app”) is designed to respect your privacy completely.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, no account system, and requests no Android permissions.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of gameplay data locally on your device only (your high score, best combo, games played, and your chosen pool skin, background, and sound/music preferences) using Android’s private app storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Network

The app does not use the internet and works fully offline.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because it collects no data, it complies with children’s privacy requirements (e.g., COPPA / GDPR-K).

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same location with a new effective date.
